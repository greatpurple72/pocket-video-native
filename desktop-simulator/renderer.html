Desktop simulator

This desktop version opens real pages, blocks popup windows, detects page videos, supports fullscreen overlay gestures, and keeps an offline list. Mouse drag acts as touch drag, so you can test the full interaction flow before installing to your phone.

  • Single click toggles controls.
  • Double click left and right seek 30 seconds.
  • Double click center toggles play and pause.
  • Hold to force temporary 1x playback.
  • Left drag changes brightness overlay, right drag changes volume.
